The Address
I

Arriving at the house.

Hidden in the quiet heart of Santa Gertrudis, Ibiza, HICH House was created as a space beyond the noise of everyday life.

Passing through the arched gateway — inspired by ancient Persian philosophy and architecture — you symbolically step from the outer world into an inner one: a place to slow down, reconnect, and simply be.

II

Stone, lime and light.

Blending modern Ibiza living with subtle Persian-oriental touches, the house is designed to nourish both body and soul.

Lime walls, travertine and filtered light hold the calm. What was added is quiet — and the garden is never more than one doorway away.

The Neighborhood
V

Close to everything, far from noise.

Based in Ibiza

Santa Gertrudis is the still heart of the island — Ibiza's bohemian, artistic centre, a world away from the noise of the coast.

A whitewashed square, galleries and long tables beneath the pines; artisan markets, pine-covered hills and hidden calas all minutes away. HICH House sits in the very middle of it — close to everything the island is loved for, kept apart from everything it isn't.
