HICH House01 · The Name
The Name

In Farsi, Hich means Nothingness.

Not emptiness, but the freedom of simply being.

Hidden in the quiet heart of Santa Gertrudis, Ibiza, HICH House was created as a space beyond the noise of everyday life. Passing through the arched gateway inspired by ancient Persian philosophy and architecture, you symbolically step from the outer world into an inner one — a place to slow down, reconnect, and simply be.
